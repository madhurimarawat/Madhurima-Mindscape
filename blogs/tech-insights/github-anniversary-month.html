🚀 4 Years on GitHub

On May 12, 2026, I complete 4 years on GitHub. I started on May 12, 2022. At that time, I was confused, curious, and honestly not very confident about what I was doing.

I uploaded simple notes. I experimented with random ideas. I was scared before pushing commits.

Slowly things changed. I started building real projects. I began maintaining repositories properly. I started thinking about documentation, structure, and consistency.

GitHub did not just improve my coding skills. It shaped my discipline, my thinking process, and my confidence.
